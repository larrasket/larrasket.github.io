I'm pretty paranoid (as mostly claimed) of coding. I always think of the art of programming in its all ways (OO, XP, etc..); it had to be done right. Maybe the reason of that was that I've seen a good deal of computers abusement. In my first job I've seen some code like: 

class slider {
  ...
}

And the so-called 'developer' needed another slider in the UI, what do you think? Another object of the same type? 

class otherslider {
  ...
}

Yes. Anyways; always write programs like if it will count for you in the doomsday. I created this entity to talk about a different thing; that is me being equally paranoid about music. Or even much more. The reason that nobody probably noticed that is that I ennoble it that I would never approach it. What upsets me about that is that I feel the same caprice of indignation seeing someone writes ugly code, when I notice somebody underestimating the greatness of a symphony or a chant. People, music is one of the greatest gifts of humanity, it is not something to 'fill' your time1 with, it's something to embrace, contemplate and deep dive into it. Having a type of music that is 'shareable' is very unusual thing; it's like having four hundred friends; a friend of everybody is a friend to none. Have a look into the aesthetic philosophy of your music, lookup your enthusiasm there, and enjoy they journey of isolated autism with music.

obviously, if you prefer to listen to hiphop, rap, underground or any other خرا mainstream; you have much bigger problem to work on.
