Books that changed me to someone else

The title is a little bit problematic or maybe misleading, but I was not able to find any better, ultimately only you can change yourself however, you might find in the following list of books some insights that possibly will influence this process of changing.

  • Les Passions de l'âme (Descartes)

    If you are going to read only one book of this list, let it be this one. I wish I've known this book much earlier, it would help me to skip many mistakes of commotion in my life. I'd like to add a note for those of you who didn't experience reading old works before, you will find this work in it's first ~40 arts very boring, that it starts by very simple definitions that you might find silly and unneeded, I will not try to justify it here but I will ask you to tolerate these parts till the end of the book. Thank me later :D.
